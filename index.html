Where can I adopt (buy) a soc???
Right here on the website. When launched, choose how many you want, and then just press the 'adopt a soc' button (gotta have metamask or some kinda wallet with Matic though...)
How much is it to adopt a soc?
When are they escaping?
Their cost is to be determined but we're looking at 130 matic or less. Since this is a small team we're essentially using these funds as a kickstarter to get things moving.
What network are socs on?
We're using Matic! Less gas = better prices = more socs = more fun...
How many socs are there???
12,000. Why that number...Because of the pair of socs system. And there are over 280 individual items so 10,000 is kind of limiting.
What is the "pair of socs" system all about?
Glad you asked. As seen on our roadmap we will be launching the 'pair of socs' system later on. This means that for every soc there will be a complimentary one. When both are owned you are granted special privileges. This is meant to jumpstart trading in the future, boost your soc investment, and get more people involved.
What happens when I adopt (mint) a soc?
When you press buy (if you're using MetaMask) you'll have to switch to the Matic network. Boom. You've now adopted a soc. They'll be associated with your wallet ID and will appear on Opensea shortly.
What is this soc game all about?
We wanted to add some real functionality to owning a soc. Meet ups are great and everything but they're not accessible to everyone. The soc game will esentially be a constant place to meetup 24/7 for soc holders. And of course the socs you own will be availble for you to use in game.
